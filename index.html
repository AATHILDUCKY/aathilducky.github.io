About Me

A bit more context about who I am and what I’m building.

Final-year BSc Computer Science · Trincomalee Campus, EUSL

I’m Aathil Ahamed, but most people in the community know me as “Aathil Ducky”. I’m a cybersecurity enthusiast & ethical hacking learner with a strong focus on offensive security, penetration testing, and web vulnerabilities.

Over time, I’ve worked with tools like Burp Suite, Nmap, Metasploit, SQLmap, Suricata, Wireshark and frameworks like Python & Django, building my own security tools, automation scripts and learning resources.

I also enjoy teaching and sharing what I learn: from YouTube tutorials, to Medium & Dev.to blogs, to running my own cybersecurity blog PwnTrends focused on real-world attacks, walkthroughs and cheat sheets.
