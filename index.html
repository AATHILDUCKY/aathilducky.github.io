About Me

A self-driven cybersecurity learner focused on offensive security, Python tooling, and sharing knowledge with the community.

Final-year BSc Computer Science · Trincomalee Campus (EUSL)

I'm Aathil Ahamed, better known online as "Aathil Ducky". I'm a Cyber Security Analyst & OffSec Enthusiast with a strong focus on offensive security, penetration testing, and web vulnerabilities.

I've spent time experimenting with tools like Burp Suite Nmap Metasploit SQLmap Suricata Wireshark and building my own utilities and labs using Python & Django. Some of my public projects include duck-duck-leaks, XSS notes, google dorks notes, and ethical hacking short notes.

I love converting learning into content — from YouTube walkthroughs, to Medium & Dev.to posts, to my own platform PwnTrends, where I write about real-world attacks, CTF-style workflows, and practical cheat sheets for beginners and students.
